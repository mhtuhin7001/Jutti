Welcome To Jano Unnayon Technical Training Institute (JUTTI)

Grameen Jano Unnayan Songstha , known as GJUS initiated Technical Training Institute in December 2022 with a view to empowering technical skill in several fields of technology to the adolescent's boys & girls of GJUS's program participants and other community members in GJUS's working areas. At the beginning its activities started from Altajer Rahaman Road, Bhola Sadar, Bhola. In Jano Unnayan Technical Training Institute (JUTTI), Bhola as per the need of our country as well as overseas requirement several new technology based short Course training facilities have been incorporated.

From 16/08/2023 first batch of three month long technical training courses in 3 (three) different trades namely, 1) Genarel Ceargiving, 2) Electrical Instalation & Maintainance (EIM) , 3) Beutification has begun. Regarding best utilization of JUTTI facilities, management has decided to remain open for all from all over the country who are interested in skill building technical education. For conducting updated technical training, education and higher professional courses as well as to produce professionally qualified and skilled manpower in our country GJUS management has taken the challenge to establish a professional technical training and higher education institute primarily in the JUTTI campus, Bhola Sadar. The name of the institute has chosen as Jano Unnayan Technical Training Institute (JUTTI). Since 2022 Janno Unnayan Technical Training Institutes (JUTTI) run under the guidance and control of GJUS. JUTTI can be considered a social enterprise as its earnings will be fully utilized for improving the institute itself. One of the major specialty of JUTTI is to provide. Education Loan for the deserving adolescent boys and girls. Qualified and well experienced trainers and expert management set up has already been engaged there for running the training institute smoothly.

Till now, trainees who have successfully completed the JUTTI technical courses, almost all of them have already been employed by various relevant organizations/agencies and some of them are self-employed.
